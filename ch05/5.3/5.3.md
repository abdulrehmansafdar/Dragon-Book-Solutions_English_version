### 5.3.2 !

Given an SDD, translate an infix expression with + and * into an expression without redundant parentheses.For example, because both operators are left associative and * has higher precedence than +, ((a\*(b+c))\*(d)) can be translated into a\*(b+c)\*d

#### Answer

Several property settings:

- wrapped: Whether there are parentheses in the outermost layer of the expression.
- precedence: Let the precedence of +, \*, () and single digit be 0, 1, 2, 3 respectively.If there are parentheses at the outermost level of the expression, it is the priority of the last operator evaluated after removing the parentheses; otherwise, it is the priority of the last operator evaluated in the expression.
- expr: expression.
- cleanExpr: Expression with redundant parentheses removed.
