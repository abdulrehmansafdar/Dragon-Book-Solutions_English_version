### 3.1.2

Here is a photo of <b>my house</b>;
<p><img src="house.gif"/><br/>

Divide into appropriate morpheme sequences.Which morphemes should have associated grammatical values?What value should it have?

#### Answer

<nodestart, p> <selfendnode, img> <selfendnode, br>
